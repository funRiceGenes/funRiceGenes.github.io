* **Key message**  
    + These observations provided further evidence that VYL plays a role in regulating chloroplast biogenesis and that VYL, OsClpP3, and OsClpP4 function together in the chloroplast Clp.
    + Notably, we found that OsClpPs, OsClpT, and OsClpR4 had similar expression patterns to VYL in wild-type plants, with peak mRNA accumulation at the early stage of chloroplast development.
